How to apply

Thank you for your interest in icddr,b. Please click on Current Opportunities tab on the upper left side menu to view currently available positions along with their application deadlines and then click on your desired job title to find the details about the position. When you click on the position you are interested in, you would be able to access the relevant information you require in order to apply. To continue, please click on Apply online button and follow the instructions. Be patient, email will be arrived within 5 minutes. Should you require any technical assistance regarding our online application system, please e-mail to it.helpdesk@icddrb.org. Please do not send your CV by e-mail. Please note that the information captured by icddr,b pertinent to employment will be dealt with in strict confidentiality and will not be published to any third party.
