About icddr,b

icddr,b, an international research institution situated in Bangladesh, is provided with a unique opportunity for research into the health challenges facing developing countries worldwide. Over 50 years icddr,b has earned its reputation as a ‘Centre of Excellence’ for its achievements in diarrhoeal diseases, nutrition, infectious diseases, population programmes and child survival strategies. National and international staff [3700] work at the main campus in Dhaka, as well as, at twenty five field sites around the country. We offer employees the opportunity to work for an organisation at the forefront of global health. icddr,b is an equal opportunity employer and particularly welcomes applications from women candidates. As a world-class research organisation, we attract and retain talents through offering competitive compensation packages and challenging career opportunities.
